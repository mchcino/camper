CORPORATE PROFILE

Who Whe Are

Founded in 2005, our travel agency began its journey by offering personalized travel packages across Canada’s most iconic destinations. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading providers of curated travel experiences in the region.

In 2010, we expanded our services to include guided adventure tours, offering thrilling excursions through Canada’s breathtaking national parks. By 2015, we introduced luxury travel options, combining world-class accommodations with immersive cultural and nature-based experiences. Today, we continue to innovate, crafting unforgettable journeys tailored to every traveler’s dream.

What You'll Get from Our Travel

A Stunning Mountain View

Starting from the Rocky Mountains, Whistler Mountain, Mount Logan, the Valley of the Ten Peaks, and many others, you are free to explore them all!

Strathcona Provincial Park

Strathcona Provincial Park on Vancouver Island is known for its ancient trees, including some over 1,000 years old, and stunning wilderness, offering activities like hiking and camping in a pristine natural environment.

Alert Bay

Alert Bay is a village on Cormorant Island, known for its indigenous Kwakwaka’wakw culture, totem poles, and the U’mista Cultural Centre. It offers visitors a unique cultural experience with a focus on traditional art and history.

Climb the Mountains

Mountain hiking in Canada offers an extraordinary adventure with spectacular views, especially in Banff and Jasper, showcasing beautiful mountain landscapes and challenging trails.

Relaxation in Hot Springs

Relax in Canada's hot springs, from Banff to Radium and Liard River, with soothing mineral waters and breathtaking mountain views.

Sleep Under the Sky

Experience the beauty of Canada's nature by spending the night under the stars with a CAMPER in places like Banff and Jasper National Parks. Enjoy scenic views and peaceful nights.
